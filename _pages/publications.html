All zeros of (super) String Theory

Yu-Chi Chang, Hsing-Yen Chen, Yu-Tin Huang
arXiv preprint arXiv:2506.15299 [hep-th] (2025)
[Download PDF] [arXiv Page]

Abstract: We develop a scaffolding technique that builds Yang-Mills and higher-level string amplitudes from tachyon amplitudes. We formulate curve-integral representations for fermionic amplitudes to identify hidden zeros in Super Yang-Mills and superstring theories.
