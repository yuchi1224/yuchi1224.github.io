[1] All zeros of (super) String Theory

Yu-Chi Chang, Hsing-Yen Chen, Yu-Tin Huang
arXiv preprint arXiv:2506.15299 [hep-th] (2025)
[Download PDF] [arXiv Page]

Abstract: Reformulated open string and superstring amplitudes using surfaceology (curve-integral representations) and scaffolding techniques to reveal their zero structure and elucidate the connection between $\text{Tr}(\phi^3)$ and Yang-Mills theory. Moreover, we also consider the gluino amplitude at four and six-points, identifying its zero and recovering super-Yang-Mills via scaffolding.
